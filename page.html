Meet the Team

Gentile Smiles

Welcome to Gentile Smiles Orthodontics! Since 2000, our orthodontist and team have provided personalized care to straighten our guests' teeth and improve their smiles and oral health. We offer a full range of orthodontic services, including clear aligners and braces, and our office features the latest technologies.

We invite you to call us today to schedule your consultation with Dr. Sim Johnson and learn more about orthodontics in South Jordan, Utah, Tampa, Florida, and Los Angeles, California. We look forward to helping you care for your smile!
